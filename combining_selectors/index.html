69th Emmy Awards

September 17th 2017

Much to most people's surprise, popular Netflix TV Show, Master Of None bagged Best Female Writer for a Comedy Series with Lena Waithe being the first ever black woman to do so.

Lena Waithe

A surprise to most was that ,arguably the most watched sci-fi series currently, WestWorld missed out on all the categories it was nominated.

A personal disappointment was that Bob Odenkirk, lead actor in Better Call Saul did not win the Best Male Actor.
